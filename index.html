Data Source


FIRMS wildfire event point shapefile data were be masked to the Paraguayan Chaco region, and to the temporal span for the study (2001-2025). The covariates with a daily temporal resolution were aggregated by getting the mean value of the preceding 6 days. In case of rasters with a coarser temporal resolution, the most recent available data were be used instead (8 and 16 days). Rasters with no temporal resolution were not averaged.


All rasters were resampled to a 500 by 500 meter scale. The data preparation and extraction were done with a Python script, using the Earth Engine module to access raster datasets, GeoPandas to ingest the FIRMS data, and the Scikit-Learn module to develop the random forest regression model.

What?

Cinque Terre comprises five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore.
